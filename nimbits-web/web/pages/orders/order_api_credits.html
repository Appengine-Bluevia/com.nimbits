You can use the public Nimbits cloud with a free quota of 1000 api calls a day. Api calls are reset every day at midnight UTC. If you want to read and write data beyond the free quota, you can fund your account by purchasing credits that will be used automatically when your account uses more than 1000 api calls each day. An api call costs approximately $0.00001, so a $20 USD block will provide about 2 million requests*.

How billing works

Nimbits uses a special data point on your account for tracking your account balance. When you exceed the daily free quota, Nimbits will being deducting from your balance.

Since your account balance is a data point, you can setup subscriptions, alerts and calculations just like any other data feed in your account. When you enable billing, Nimbits automatically creates three subscriptions for you to alert you by email if your account hits zero, exceeds a daily quota you set, or is funded and your account balance increases.

You can use the Nimbits console to configure a maximum quota for each day so your account will not exceed a set amount.

*Please note that a cost of $0.00001 per api call is an approximation and costs may vary slightly depending on the nature of the request. Rates are subject to change.
