The Nimbits Android App allows you to create data points on the Nimbits cloud and view your data feeds, manually enter data, and view alerts. We are adding features to this app every day to let you manage your feeds from here, in the meantime you can login to the Nimbits Web Console to configure your alerts, calculations, etc.

To get started, login to Nimbits Web and create some data feeds, or create your some from here using the menu

What's New

  • Login to the nimbits console to create an alert for high, low values, or any other event for your data feed and select Android Push Notification to have your alerts pushed to your phone or tablet.
