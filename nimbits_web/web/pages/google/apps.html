Nimbits for Google Apps™

Nimbits gives you a collection of APIs you can use to record software and sensor data directly to the cloud, or upload your existing process control data generated from your internal systems. Once on the cloud, you can use our powerful tools to work with and share your feeds.

Nimbits can be added to your domain from the Google Apps Marketplace™ by a domain administrator and used throughout your organisation to share and visualize sensor and process data. Nimbits works with both the free edition of Google Apps™ and Google Apps for Business™."

When you add Nimbits to your Domain, you get:

  • A link to Nimbits in Google's universal navigation bar
  • Single Sign on for all of your domain users
  • The ability to export logged point data to your Google Drive™
  • Merge, analyse and visualise exported data with Google Fusion Tables™
